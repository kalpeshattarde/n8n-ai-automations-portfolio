# Business Case

Inbound leads lose value when qualification and follow-up depend on manual review. This project shows how an AI-assisted RevOps workflow can turn new form submissions into prioritized sales actions.

## Business Value

- Faster first response for high-intent leads.
- Clear lead tiering for owners and sales managers.
- Reduced manual review of low-fit inquiries.
- Drafted follow-ups that keep humans in control before sending.
- Daily manager report with revenue-risk and time-saved signals.

## ROI Model

The local report estimates time saved by replacing manual lead review, note drafting, and daily reporting. In production, this can be tied to meeting booked rate, speed-to-lead, and opportunity conversion.
