# Google Sheet Columns

This workflow expects a Google Form response sheet and a separate `Proposal Log` tab.

## Original Form Columns

* Timestamp
* Email address
* Full name
* Work email
* Contact No
* Your Role in Business
* Company name
* Website(Optional)
* Industry
* Company size
* Monthly revenue
* Country
* Tools you currently use
* How does your operation run today?
* Where is the biggest pain?
* Estimated hours lost per week to manual work
* Your team's AI experience
* Existing automations
* Data readiness
* Which workflows would you like AI to handle?
* Desired outcomes
* Timeline
* Budget
* Decision authority
* Anything else we should know?
* How did you hear about us?
* Upload any helpful files
* Contact permission agreement

## Proposal Log Columns

* Timestamp
* Full name
* Work email
* Contact No
* Company name
* Industry
* Country
* Lead Score
* Lead Type

* Proposal Status
