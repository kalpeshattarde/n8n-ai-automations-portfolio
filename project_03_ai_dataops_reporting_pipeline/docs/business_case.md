# Business Case

Leadership reporting breaks down when order data is inconsistent, delayed, or manually cleaned in spreadsheets. This project demonstrates a local DataOps pipeline that turns raw orders into reliable KPIs and a stakeholder-ready dashboard.

## Business Value

- Faster weekly revenue and collections reporting.
- Cleaner data quality visibility before metrics reach leadership.
- Overdue invoice and pending payment visibility.
- Delayed order detection for operations follow-up.
- Reproducible KPI calculations from raw CSV rows.

## ROI Model

The simulation estimates reporting time saved by automating cleaning, KPI calculation, insight generation, and dashboard creation. In production, this should be measured against analyst hours, reporting cycle time, and collections follow-up rate.
